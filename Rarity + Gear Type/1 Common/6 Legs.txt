How to Roll

Roll a d10 and a d100, minus 1 from the d10.

The d10 is the 100s place and the d100 is the tens and ones.

E.g. if you roll a 10 (-1) and a 56 that's 396.

If you roll a 1 (-1) and a 04 that's 4.

1 (-1) and a 00 becomes 1000.
